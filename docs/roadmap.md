## 3. Future Expansion Goals

### 3.1 Edge Intelligence
- Enable **on-device AI inference** using DuckDB + WebGPU.  
- Support federated machine learning with privacy-preserving data collaboration.  
- Extend SDK compatibility to **IoT gateways and embedded systems**.

### 3.2 Interoperability
- Implement **cross-chain bridges** (Arbitrum, Polygon, Sui) for multi-network token utility.  
- Integrate decentralized identity (DID) and verifiable credentials for secure data provenance.

### 3.3 Research & Academic Collaboration
- Partner with universities for **ZK-proof research** and distributed systems optimization.  
- Publish open academic papers under DuckFi Labs.

### 3.4 Community Growth
- Host global **DuckFi Hackathons** and developer grants.  
- Launch Ambassador and Builder Programs in strategic regions (SEA, EU, LATAM).  
- Support educational content creators and DAO-led technical workshops.

### 3.5 Sustainability
- Transition to **fully DAO-funded model** by 2028.  
- DAO treasury yields sustain open-source development and grants indefinitely.  
- Maintain net-neutral emissions through token buybacks and burn policies.
